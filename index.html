Understanding the Technology

Agentic AI

  • Autonomous systems that can plan and execute tasks
  • Capable of making decisions with minimal human input
  • Can break down complex problems into sub-tasks
  • Potential to operate continuously without supervision

RAG Chatbots

  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines LLMs with external knowledge
  • Can access and reference up-to-date information
  • Reduces hallucinations compared to pure LLMs
  • Easily customized with domain-specific data

Considerations & Challenges

Agentic AI Challenges

  • Requires careful goal alignment
  • Potential for unpredictable behaviors
  • Higher computational costs
  • Need for robust monitoring systems

RAG Limitations

  • Dependent on quality of knowledge base
  • Retrieval can introduce latency
  • Still prone to some hallucination
  • Requires good document chunking strategy
